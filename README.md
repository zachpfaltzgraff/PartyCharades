## 🚀 About the Project

Party Charades was designed, developed, polished, and published to the **Apple App Store by a single developer in under 24 hours** as a personal challenge.

Despite the rapid development timeline, the app delivers a polished gameplay experience with customizable settings, offline support, and an intuitive interface built entirely with Flutter.

---

## ✨ Features

- 🎭 Classic head-tilt gameplay
- 📝 Create unlimited custom decks
- 📤 Import and export custom decks
- 🎲 16 built-in themed decks

- 🔊 Optional sound effects

## 🎮 How to Play

1. Choose a deck.
2. Hold your phone against your forehead.
3. Your teammates describe or act out the word.
4. Tilt your phone:
   - ⬇️ **Correct**
   - ⬆️ **Skip**
5. Score as many points as possible before time runs out!

Simple to learn, endlessly replayable.
